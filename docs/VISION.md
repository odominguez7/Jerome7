# Jerome7 — Vision & Manifesto

## The Thesis

**Commitment compounds. Everything is possible. YU matter.**

Jerome7 is not a fitness app. It is an act of love disguised as seven minutes.

---

## Omar's Story

Omar is a builder at MIT who watched his generation collapse under the weight of productivity culture. Not from laziness — from caring too much about too many things and never learning how to show up for themselves first.

He watched his friend Miguel — brilliant, driven, the kind of person who would stay up until 4am building something beautiful — slowly stop showing up. Not to class. Not to meals. Not to life. Miguel did not fail because he lacked talent. He failed because nobody taught him that the smallest commitment, repeated daily, is the most powerful force in the universe.

Jerome7 exists because of Miguel. Because of every builder who has burned out in silence.

---

## The Problem

Gen Z is the most burned-out generation in history:

- **42%** of Gen Z report feeling burned out (Deloitte, 2023)
- **1 in 3** college students report clinically significant anxiety
- The average attention span for sustained effort has dropped below the length of a YouTube Short
- Fitness app retention at 30 days: **4%**

The industry's response? More features. More gamification. More noise.

Jerome7's response: **seven minutes. Every day. Same session for everyone on earth.**

---

## The Architecture of Commitment

Jerome7 is built on a single insight: **the barrier to showing up is not motivation — it is decision fatigue.**

When you open Jerome7, there is nothing to choose. No workout library. No customization. No algorithm optimizing for engagement. There is one session. Seven blocks. Sixty seconds each. The same for every human on earth that day.

Like Wordle, but for your body.

This is not a limitation. It is the product.

---

## The Rules

1. Show up = 7 minutes. That is it.
2. Miss 1 day? Chain holds.
3. Miss 2 days? Still holds. Life happens.
4. Miss 3? Chain breaks. Start over.
5. 1 save per 30 days. Use it when you need it.
6. Milestones at 7, 14, 30, 50, 100, 200, 365.
7. Longest streak never resets. That is your record.

---

## The Agents

Five AI agents coordinate behind the scenes:

- **Coach** — Generates today's session. Bodyweight, fun, surprising. Never repeats yesterday.
- **Nudge** — Learns when you are about to skip. Messages you before you break the chain. Never shames.
- **Streak** — Tracks consistency, not perfection. Saves for life.
- **Community** — Matches you with 3-5 builders at your level. Your pod. Your crew.
- **Scheduler** — Finds the window where showing up is easiest. Learns your habits.
