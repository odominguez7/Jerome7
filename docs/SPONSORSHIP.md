# Jerome7 — Sponsorship Guide

## Why Sponsor Jerome7?

Jerome7 is free forever. No premium tier. No ads. No paywall. That means it runs on the generosity of people who believe showing up matters.

Omar is investing $1,000 of his own money to launch Jerome7. Every dollar is tracked publicly.

## In-Kind Sponsorship (Technology Partners)

Companies that donate API credits, hosting, or infrastructure are recognized as **Technology Partners**.

Benefits:
- Logo on the Jerome7 README
- Logo on jerome7.com
- Mention in all launch and press materials
- Direct line to the engineering team

Interested? Reach out on [Discord](https://discord.gg/5AZP8DbEJm) or open a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/odominguez7/Jerome7/issues).
