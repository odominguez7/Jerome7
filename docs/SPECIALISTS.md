# Jerome7 — Specialist Contributors

## Who We Need

Jerome7 takes mental health and physical safety seriously. We are actively looking for specialists to review, advise, and contribute to the platform.

We welcome:
- **Licensed therapists and counselors** — Review nudge messaging and burnout detection logic
- **Certified fitness professionals** — Validate session content for safety and effectiveness
- **Mindfulness and meditation practitioners** — Contribute breathing and reset scripts
- **Sports psychologists** — Advise on streak psychology and motivation systems
- **Public health researchers** — Help us measure impact responsibly

---

## How to Contribute

### 1. Content Review
Review AI-generated session content for physical safety. Flag exercises that could cause injury without proper form cues. Suggest modifications for different ability levels.

### 2. Mindfulness & Breathing Scripts
The "Reset" phase of each session includes breathing and grounding exercises. Specialists can contribute scripts that are evidence-based and accessible to beginners.

### 3. Nudge Messaging
The Nudge agent sends messages when users are at risk of breaking their streak. Specialists help ensure these messages are encouraging, never shaming, and psychologically sound.

### 4. Burnout Detection
Jerome7 tracks patterns that may indicate burnout (sudden streak breaks, declining engagement). Specialists advise on how to respond appropriately and when to suggest professional resources.

### 5. Research Collaboration
If you are a researcher studying habit formation, Gen Z wellness, or digital health interventions, we would love to collaborate. Jerome7 generates anonymized, aggregate data that could support published research.

## Guidelines

- All contributions must be evidence-based
- No promotional content or affiliate links
- Patient/client confidentiality must always be maintained
- Jerome7 is not a substitute for professional care — we make this clear to all users
