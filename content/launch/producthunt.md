## Maker Comment

Hey Product Hunt! I'm Omar -- MIT student who lost 80 lbs by showing up for 7 minutes a day.

Jerome7 is the system I wish I had when I started. One session per day. Same for everyone on Earth. No decisions, no browsing exercises, no subscription.

What makes it different:

**5 AI agents that coordinate behind the scenes:**
- Coach generates sessions via Gemini 2.5 Flash and adapts from your feedback
- Nudge predicts when you'll skip and intervenes before your chain breaks
- Streak uses a 3-miss rule (not binary -- one bad day doesn't erase 47 good ones)
- Community matches you into accountability pods of 3-5 builders
- Scheduler finds your optimal session window

**MCP-native:** Jerome7 is an MCP server. Your AI assistant (Claude, GPT, etc.) can check your chain, pull today's session, and log completions through standard tool calls.

**The globe:** Every builder who shows up becomes a dot on a live 3D globe. Watch the world light up at jerome7.com/globe.

**AI voice:** ElevenLabs narrates each block live -- fully guided experience.

**From your terminal:** `npx jerome7`. That's it.

Personally funded. No premium tier. No ads. Apache 2.0. I'm investing $1K of my own money into this community, tracked publicly.

Built because 86% of Gen Z report burnout before 25. Seven minutes is the intervention.

YU matter.
