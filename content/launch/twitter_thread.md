**Tweet 3 (The 5 agents):**

Under the hood: 5 AI agents running autonomously.

Coach -- generates today's session, adapts from your feedback
Nudge -- predicts when you'll skip, intervenes before you break
Streak -- 3-miss chain rule (not binary -- because 1 bad day shouldn't erase 47 good ones)
Community -- matches you into pods of 3-5
Scheduler -- finds your optimal window

---

**Tweet 4 (Agent mesh):**

The interesting part: these agents talk to each other.

Coach reads your chain state and skip patterns.
Nudge reads your Scheduler windows.
Community reads everyone's data to form pods.

No centralized orchestrator. A mesh of agents coordinating to keep you consistent.

This is what agentic AI should look like.
